Welcome To RTB!

Embark on a rewarding career as a US IT recruiter, domestic IT recruiter, or non-IT recruiter. RTB is your destination for comprehensive training programs, supporting professionals at all levels.

Our training programs offer practical exposure in recruitment, emphasizing HR and sales. From newcomers to seasoned professionals, we assist in finding employment until you secure a position.

Recruiters, regardless of background, benefit from our programs. Prerequisites include a degree, good English communication skills, and a basic understanding of technical knowledge. Refreshers cover topics like effective communication, office dynamics, soft skills development, resume crafting, interview preparation, and mock questions.

About

Learn Recruitment concepts, become a Professional Recruiter covering HR or IT/Non-IT for Domestic/International roles. Explore commonly asked questions by job seekers for a unique synthesis of methods and techniques.
